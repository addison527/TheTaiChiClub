The Art of Yin and Yang

The Yin-Yang theory is an early precursor to theory of relativity. yin and yang is a unity of opposite. World is composed of opposites, like water and stone, one of the softest and one of the hardest. Flowing Water can break stone and stone can block flowing water. Together a rock mountain surrounding by water or vice versa is, a balance of the opposite, often a harmonious beautiful scene.
